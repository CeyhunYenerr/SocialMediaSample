Welcome to this study

You will complete a number of simple tasks, together with other people,
with whom you will be connected via the internet.

First, all participants in the study will select personal "avatars" and
write short texts to introduce themselves.

Before beginning the tasks, you will spend 3 minutes with the other
people. During this time, you will be able to read and react to
each-other's brief introductions.

Specific instructions will follow.

Thank you!
